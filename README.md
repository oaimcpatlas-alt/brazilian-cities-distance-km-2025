# Brazilian cities distance in kilometers

As of 2025, the two most populous cities in Brazil are:
- São Paulo
- Rio de Janeiro

Using the coordinates from their Wikipedia pages and the haversine formula:
- São Paulo: 23.550°S, 46.633°W
- Rio de Janeiro: 22.91111°S, 43.20556°W
- Great-circle distance: 357.34 km

Comparison with the USA:
- United States top two cities: New York City and Los Angeles
- Great-circle distance: 3936.37 km

Conclusion:
The distance between the two most populous cities in the USA is higher than the distance between the two most populous cities in Brazil.
